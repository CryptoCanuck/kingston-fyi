# Kingston.FYI — Hyperlocal Landscape Research Digest

_Compiled 2026-05-30 via web research. Grounds the product brief; full depth feeds the PRD._

## 1. Kingston, ON incumbents & comparables

**Local news**
- **Kingstonist** (kingstonist.com) — Dominant independent local outlet; locally owned;
  Qualified Canadian Journalism Organization. Covers news + events + arts/culture +
  restaurants + sports/rec; active social. **Closest thing to the cross-linked model and the
  primary competitor** — but blends news with editorial "what to do/where to eat" lists, not
  a structured claimable directory/events database.
- **YGK News** (ygknews.ca) — Smaller independent; grew from a CFRC radio show; strong social.
- **Kingston Whig-Standard** (Postmedia) — Oldest continuously publishing daily in Canada
  (1834); now ~4 days/week; hollowed-out local newsroom — a gap opportunity.
- **Kingston Daily** (kingstondaily.ca) — Radio-group-affiliated; modest. (ownership unverified)
- National/regional: Global News Kingston, CTV Ottawa/Kingston — broadcast, not hyperlocal.

**Events**
- **Visit Kingston / Tourism Kingston** (visitkingston.ca/events) — Polished but visitor-facing,
  not resident-utility.
- **Downtown Kingston BIA** (downtownkingston.ca) — **Already runs the integrated model in
  miniature**: searchable business directory + events calendar + content ("The Downtown Dish"),
  cross-linked — but **downtown BIA members only**.
- City of Kingston calendar — civic/official; bureaucratic.

**Directory**
- Downtown Kingston BIA (best UX, downtown-only) · Greater Kingston Chamber (members only) ·
  Kingston Economic Development / investkingston.ca (B2B/investment, not consumer).

**Cross-link verdict:** Only Downtown BIA truly cross-links — and only for one neighbourhood's
members. **No incumbent offers a city-wide, structured, claimable, cross-linked hub. Open lane.**

## 2. The hyperlocal model broadly

- **KingstonToday does NOT exist** — Village Media runs 25+ Ontario "Today" sites but none in
  Kingston; the city is uncovered by them. (verify domain before finalizing)
- **Village Media** = proven Canadian small-city model. ~70% revenue from **direct local
  display ads** (on-the-ground sales) + programmatic + small directory + sponsored content +
  email sponsorship. No paywall; voluntary contributions. **New markets take 2–3 years to
  profitability**, then ~20–25% gross margin. Standard banners, no pop-ups.
- **Patch (US)** = cautionary + recovery. Burned millions at 900 sites under AOL; near-collapse
  2013. Turned profitable by stripping cost (automation), requiring ~$4–5k ad minimums
  (hand-selling mom-and-pops didn't scale), and self-serve event/announcement promotion.
  Lesson: **"you can't nationalize local news"** — readers demand local.
- **Fits a small city:** direct local display ads (anchor, labour-heavy), **newsletter
  sponsorship** (high-value/low-effort), **featured/promoted + claim listing tiers** (recurring,
  self-serve), event-promo fees, voluntary memberships. Hard paywalls underperform for hyperlocal.

## 3. Business-directory dynamics

- **Acquisition:** scrape/seed + owner **claim flows** + submissions — combine all. Seed via
  scrape, convert owners with a claim flow that gates the premium/featured tier (monetization +
  accuracy in one motion).
- **Accuracy = the moat.** Consistent NAP is a Google local-ranking signal; ~56% of consumers
  expect the business's own listing to be most accurate. Stale data kills trust.
- **Vs Google Business Profile / Yelp:** can't beat Google on discovery head-on. Win only by
  offering what Google doesn't — curation, local editorial context, cross-linking to
  news/events, community trust, niche filters. "A thinner Google has no reason to exist."
- **Revenue benchmark:** niche directories ~$100–500/mo; established $5k–$50k+/mo.

## 4. SEO / discovery

- **News SEO** (fresh original local reporting) → Google News/Discover; the volume engine.
- **Event rich results:** valid Event JSON-LD surfaces in Google "Events near me" carousel,
  often above the Map Pack; rich results ~58% of clicks. High-leverage, low-cost.
- **"Near me"/hyperlocal** queries convert high; LocalBusiness schema + neighbourhood content.
- **Cross-linking is itself an SEO asset** — internal news→event→business links build authority.

## 5. Risks & success factors (small team, ~130k city)

- **Runway:** proven model takes 2–3 yrs to profitability even with sales staff.
- **Sales labour:** direct local ad sales is the anchor but most time-consuming; favor
  self-serve directory/claim tiers + newsletter sponsorship over bespoke selling.
- **Content treadmill:** daily original news drives traffic but is unsustainable solo.
- **Incumbent (Kingstonist):** differentiate on structured/claimable directory + events utility,
  not on out-reporting day one.
- **Success factor:** stay relentlessly local; lean on scraping/automation for directory seeding
  so human effort concentrates on news + relationships.

## Top implications for the brief

1. **The cross-linked, city-wide, claimable directory IS the differentiator** — lead with the
   integration, not "another news site."
2. **Monetize directory + newsletter first**, not bespoke ad sales (solo constraints + Patch lesson).
3. **Event-schema + LocalBusiness structured data from day one** — cheap, high-leverage discovery.
4. **Plan a 2–3 yr runway + sustainable cadence** (curated + scraped + selective original).
5. **Win against Google by being what Google isn't** — curated, contextual, cross-linked, trusted.
